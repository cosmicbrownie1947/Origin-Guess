Privacy Policy

Origin Guess is an educational game that does not collect personal information.

Information We Collect

Usernames and scores are stored to provide a leaderboard feature. This information is not linked to real identities.

Children's Privacy

This game is not intended to collect personal data from children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 in the European Union (GDPR-K). Users are encouraged not to enter real names or personal information.

Data Storage

Scores are stored in a database and usernames are stored locally in your browser.

Parental Guidance

If you are under 13, please ask a parent or guardian before playing.

Contact

This project is for educational purposes and does not collect identifiable user data.
